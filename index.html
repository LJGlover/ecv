About Me

I am new to the tech industry and am a career-changer. Previously, I have worked in international adult education in the private sector as a Corporate Language Trainer which I enjoyed for almost 15 years. However, I chose to leave that career in 2016 and since then have run my own business as a dog trainer after following my lifelong passion for our canine friends. That, however, ended with the Covid-19 pandemic as it was no longer possible to go into people's houses. This, I felt, was the ideal time to retrain in a field I have always loved as a user, and that is tech. Now I am looking to start my new career with an entry-level position in Cloud Computing. My tech interests include solutions architecture, IoT, databases, and analytics.

Education & Courses Completed

I graduated from the University of Hull with a 2:1 triple honours degree in modern languages and linguistics. The tech courses I have completed so far include COMPTIA A+; COMPTIA Network+; COMPTIA Security+; and Gamification. I am currently close to completing the AWS re/Start programme.

Work Experience

I have had the good fortune to live and work around the world while working as a language trainer in the UK, Europe, and Asia. My career started as a novice language teacher while still at university - and without any teaching qualifications - and over the course of 10 years I rose to be the Head of Academic Departments in various companies. Now I am looking to repeat that success in the world of tech. I will be starting out as an AWS Cloud Practitioner and my ambition is to once again become a leader of teams and departments - or indeed companies! - while making a substantial contribution to their successes.
